Expansion based on Справочник клинических статусов [РФ] v0.1.0 (CodeSystem)

All codes in this table are from the system http://fhir.ru/fhir/CodeSystem/fhir-ru-cs-clinicalstatus

LvlCodeDisplayDefinition
0activeактивноеThe subject is currently experiencing the symptoms of the condition or there is evidence of the condition.
1  recurrenceрекуррентноеThe subject is experiencing a re-occurence or repeating of a previously resolved condition, e.g. urinary tract infection, pancreatitis, cholangitis, conjunctivitis.
1  relapseрецидивThe subject is experiencing a return of a condition, or signs and symptoms after a period of improvement or remission, e.g. relapse of cancer, multiple sclerosis, rheumatoid arthritis, systemic lupus erythematosus, bipolar disorder, [psychotic relapse of] schizophrenia, etc.
0inactiveнеактивноеThe subject is no longer experiencing the symptoms of the condition or there is no longer evidence of the condition.
1  remissionремиссияThe subject is no longer experiencing the symptoms of the condition, but there is a risk of the symptoms returning.
1  resolvedперенесённоеThe subject is no longer experiencing the symptoms of the condition and there is a negligible perceived risk of the symptoms returning.
